Garret Finnegan

Managing Director · Finnegan 5 Design and Engineering Ltd

Gareth founded F5 Engineering to bring specialist fire safety and compliance expertise to Ireland's construction sector. With deep knowledge of Irish building regulations and a hands-on approach to every project, Gareth leads F5's delivery across all five core disciplines.


His background spans fire safety engineering, disability access consultancy, and health & safety coordination — giving clients a single, trusted point of contact for their compliance needs.


Gareth works directly with architects, developers, and project teams to ensure compliance is integrated from the earliest design stages, preventing costly issues and keeping projects on schedule.

Our Story

F5 Engineering was founded with a clear purpose: to provide Ireland's construction industry with expert, responsive, and detail-oriented compliance consultancy.


Based in Ardee, Co. Louth, we work with developers, architects, and project teams across the country — delivering fire safety engineering, disability access consultancy, health & safety coordination, and design support from early concept through to final handover.


Our name reflects our approach. F5 — a fresh perspective on every project. We believe compliance shouldn't be an afterthought or a bottleneck. It should be integrated, clear, and solution-focused from the start.

Our Approach

We pride ourselves on being responsive, detail-oriented, and solution-focused. Every project receives dedicated attention — no matter the scale or complexity.


Where others see regulatory hurdles, we see opportunities to add value. Our integrated approach means fire safety, accessibility, and health & safety are considered together, reducing risk and avoiding costly redesigns.


We provide clear, concise, and reliable consultancy throughout the project lifecycle — giving you confidence that your project meets every requirement.
